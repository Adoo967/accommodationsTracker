# Accommodations Tracker

This is a simple web-based accommodations tracker. You can use it to track, save, and download accommodations data directly in your browser.

## How to Download and Use

1. **Download the `index.html` File**
   - Locate the `index.html` file.
   - Click on the file, then click the **Raw** button.
   - Right-click anywhere on the page and choose **Save As...** to save the file to your computer.

2. **Open in Chrome**
   - Find the downloaded `index.html` file on your computer.
   - Double-click the file, or right-click and choose **Open With > Google Chrome**.
   - The tracker will open in your Chrome browser and is ready to use.

## Saving Your Data

- **Data is stored in your browser's local storage.**
- You can save your progress incrementally by clicking the **Save Locally** button at the top of the page.
- To back up your data, use the **Save Locally** button and download the JSON file.
- You can restore your data later using the **Load from last save** button and selecting your saved JSON file.

## Important Note

- **If you clear your browser cache or local storage, your data will be lost.**
- To avoid losing your data, regularly use the **Save Locally** feature and keep a backup of your JSON file.
